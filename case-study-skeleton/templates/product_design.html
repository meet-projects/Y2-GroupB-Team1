Features And Specs

Features

The TravlerPack envelope-shaped sleeping bag is a multi-purpose solution for cold winters, featuring a full length two way zipper for an opening from both sides and for turning it into a blanket,has the ability connect multiple bags, foot zipper, draft collar, both outer pockets and specialized outer storage pockets, including a built-in pillow pocket and a waterproof document pocket, and when not in use, the TravlerPack fits snugly into its stuff sack, and can be carried using the build in handle, carry handle.

Specs

Temperature Rating: 15F (≈-9.44C)

Weight: 3.68 lbs (≈1.67 kg)

Size: 30.5 by 80 inches (≈77.5 by 203 cm)

Fill Weight: 35.3 oz (≈1000 g)

Fill Power: 650

Insulation Type: duck down

Lining: 10-denier ripstop nylon
