ABOUT US

OUR STORY

TravlerPack was founded with the aim of assisting millions of people displaced during the Syrian civil war, particularly during harsh weather conditions. Initially envisioned as a sleeping bag to provide warmth, the team decided to transform it into a disaster resilience tool after consulting with refugees about their needs. Over the past two years, they developed and tested prototypes, gathered feedback from refugees and nonprofits globally, and successfully raised $52,000 through a campaign.

Since their first distribution in winter 2017, TravlerPack has received positive feedback from recipients, validating its positive impact on people's lives. The team remains committed to continuing their mission and helping those in need during upcoming winters.
